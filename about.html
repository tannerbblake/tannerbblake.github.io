The Agawam High School Pickleball Club was founded with the mission to provide students with a fun and engaging way to participate in positive physical activity while learning valuable cooperative skills. Our goal with the club is to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels like a valuable part of the group, fostering a sense of belonging and community to all students who choose to be involved. Through regular practice and friendly competition, students develop their athletic abilities and enjoy the thrill of the game. We hope that our club is able to emphasize the importance of good sportsmanship inside and outside of the courts, teaching respect, integrity, and teamwork to all students involved. The AHS Pickleball Club was founded by Agawam Student Tanner Blake who realized how the club could be beneficial to the AHS community through the medium of an activity that students already love.
