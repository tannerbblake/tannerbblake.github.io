Pickleball At AHS

According to the 2024 Sports & Fitness Industry Association (SFIA) Topline Participation Report, Pickleball is the fastest-growing sport in the America, yet it had not found its way into Agawam High School until we decided to change that. Many AHS students have grown to love playing Pickleball, and for that reason we decided to start the AHS Pickleball club as an afterschool club in which students can congregate on the courts and have fun playing a physically invigorating and cooperative sport.

Why Pickleball?

With its extremely rapid growth throughout the last few years, Pickleball has become a favorite activity of many students yet is marginally underrepresented in public high schools throughout the country due to its recent emergence. Through the creation of the Agawam High School Pickleball Club, we have ensured that students have the opportunity to participate in a game of ever increasing societal significance. Most importantly, our club provides a setting conducive of good physical fitness, cooperation between teammates and positive sportsmanship amongst student-athletes, all of which help to create a better community at Agawam High School.

Join In!

Any AHS students who want to join the Pickleball Club are more than welcome and we would love to have you! The AHS Pickleball club meets at the fenced in courts that run parallel to Mill Street right after school and all are welcome. There is no cap on the quantity of people allowed and no requirement that you come to every meeting or even more than one meeting, so feel free to bring a friend or join in just once to see what we're all about! With any questions or concerns, please reach out to us through our points of contact which can be found on our Contact Page.
